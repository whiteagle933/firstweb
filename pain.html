Nociception and Pain

  • Pain - an unpleasant sensory and emotional experience associated with actual or potential tissue damage, or described in terms of such damage (International Association for the Study of Pain)

  • Nociception - the physiological process involved in the transduction of a noxious stimulus (one strong enough to signify tissue damage) and the conduction of impulses from the periphery to the cortex

  • Difference: pain is a perception
